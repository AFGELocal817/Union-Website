Community Outreach Committee


The VFW on Leestown Rd. has been kind enough to provide their meeting hall for Local 817 to conduct business when not in use by the VFW.

Due to their generosity the Union is volunteering their time to assist with projects and upkeep that are ongoing at the VFW Hall.

If you are interested in pitching in please reach out to the Eboard.
