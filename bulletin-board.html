December Union Meeting - December 23, 2025

Due to a water maintenance issue at the VFW, today's (12/23/2025) regularly scheduled meeting could not be held. We will instead hold a special meeting on January 8, 2025 at 4:15 p.m. at the VFW on Leestown Road.


This meeting will focus on discussion and a vote on the 2026 budget, in light of the agency's January 12th deadline to respond to the preliminary injunction. Thank you, and we hope to see you then.
